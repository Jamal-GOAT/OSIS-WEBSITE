WELCOME TO OSIS 2025-2026 ELYON CHRISTIAN SCHOOL

What is OSIS?

OSIS is an organization that represents students and works to improve the school environment. It plays a significant role in student life as it can teach them leadership skills and help them become more involved in school activities. It also can help them build communication and teamwork skills to accomplish their goals.

Our Values

01

Leadership

We believe leadership means taking responsibility, making thoughtful decisions, and setting a positive example for others. OSIS leaders are expected to act with integrity, confidence, and accountability in every situation, both inside and outside the school environment.

02

Integrity

We believe integrity means being honest, fair, and consistent in all actions. OSIS members are expected to uphold the highest standards of ethical behavior, both in their personal lives and within the school community.

03

Respect

We believe respect means treating others with dignity and consideration. OSIS members are expected to show respect for all individuals, regardless of differences in background, beliefs, or opinions.

04

Responsibility

We believe responsibility means being accountable for one's actions and commitments. OSIS members are expected to take ownership of their roles and contribute meaningfully to the school community.

What Do We Do As A Student Council Member?

Being a student council member isn't just about the title, but real actions that can impact the school environment and create a fun events that can also help them gain new knowledge. We motivate each other to do our best to make creative events such as OCM (Osis Class Meeting), Heroes Day, and so much more events for us to plan. Our goal is to work as a compact team, strengthen our friendships, and gain new experiences that can be useful for the future.
